Product Details

The scanner uses the browser camera APIs for preview and the native `BarcodeDetector` when the browser supports it. For best results, open this page over HTTPS and use the rear camera on mobile.

Allow Camera Access

This app needs permission to use your camera sensor so it can show a live preview and scan barcodes. Choose "Allow Camera" to open the browser permission popup.
